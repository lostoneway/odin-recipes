Enchiladas

Description

Here's an easy beef enchiladas recipes that I often make. It can be made during the week or the weekend, really it's easy.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • 1 cup of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1-1 1/2 cup of Mexican Crumbling Cheese
  • 14oz can of Red Enchilada Sauce
  • 1lb of Beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. While the oven is preheating:
    • Brown beef in a medium skillet. I use lean, you know keeping it healthy.
    • Once beef is browned. Drain.
    • Return beef to skillet, pour in 1 cup of the enchilada sauce. Stirring throughly.
    • Go ahead and crumble your Mexican cheese into a bowl and set aside.
  3. In a large Pyrex dish, pour a little of the enchilada sauce on the bottom.
  4. Scoop some of your beef mixture into a tortillas, add crumbled Mexican cheese. Remember not too much or you won't be able to roll your tortillas.
  5. Place newly rolled tortillas, seam side down in your dish.
  6. Continue constructing enchiladas and placing them your dish.
  7. Once pan is full, pour remaining enchilada sauce on top. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over dish.
  8. Place enchiladas in oven for 30 minutes. Sauce and cheese should be bubbling nicely before you take them out.
  9. Remove from oven and enjoy! You could wait a few mins so you don't sorch your mouth but hey...YOLO!
