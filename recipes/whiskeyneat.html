Whiskey Neat

Description

Who doesn't love whiskey? Sometimes ice and mixers just get in the way.

Ingredients

  • Some gud, gud. Whiskey of course. Tennessee in this case!
  • Glassware

Steps

  1. Place clean glass on counter.
  2. Open bottle of your finest Tennessee whiskey.
    • Fun fact! If the spirit is made in Kentucky, it's Bourbon. But if its made in Tennessee, it's Whiskey.
    • That's just what I heard from my wife. I didn't make the rules. She's from Kentucky so I believe her.
  3. Go ahead, wrap two fingers around the glass and pour the whiskey until you reach the top of your second finger.
  4. Give it a moment and allow the whiskey to breathe.
  5. Enjoy!
